Learning Spaces & Facilities

Learning Spaces

Our Sensory-Friendly Classrooms UAE provide a calm and comfortable setting where children can learn at their own pace. Flexible seating, interactive learning resources, and a supportive atmosphere help children feel confident and ready to participate.

Therapy & Support

Children receive speech therapy, occupational therapy, behaviour therapy, and individual support in dedicated therapy rooms UAE. Our experienced team works closely with each child to help them develop communication, independence, and everyday life skills.

Learning Resource Centre

Our resource centre includes books, sensory materials, educational games, and digital learning tools that support learning, creativity, and skill development for children with different learning needs.

Play & Activity Area

Our indoor and outdoor activity spaces give children opportunities to play, explore, build friendships, and develop motor skills in a safe and supervised environment.

Safe & Caring Environment

As a trusted disability support school UAE, we provide a welcoming campus where children feel safe, respected, and supported. Our trained staff and child-friendly facilities help create a positive learning environment for every child.

Accessible Learning Environment

Our facilities are planned to meet the needs of every learner, providing an inclusive environment where children learn comfortably, participate confidently, and enjoy every stage of their journey.
