About GEIA Inclusive Academy

Fifteen years of building an education around each child, not the other way around.

Our story

Started at a kitchen table, grown into a second home for 100+ families

GEIA Inclusive Academy has two special educators, unable to find a program that treated their students as individuals rather than diagnoses, opened a single classroom in Kozhikode. Word travelled through parent networks long before it travelled through advertising.

Today we're a full multidisciplinary academy — speech-language pathologists, occupational therapists, behaviour specialists and special educators working from one shared plan for every child, not four separate ones.

Mission: to give every child, regardless of how they learn, an education built around their strengths.
Vision: a Kerala where no family has to search far, or wait long, for support that actually fits their child.

What drives us

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide inclusive, compassionate, and individualized education and therapeutic support that empowers every child to develop confidence, independence, communication skills, and lifelong learning abilities in a safe and nurturing environment.

Our Vision

To become a leading centre of excellence for autism education and individual learning support, inspiring children, families, and communities through innovation, inclusion, and meaningful learning opportunities.

How we work

Our Philosophy

Individual Learning

No two plans in our academy look alike, because no two children learn alike.

Compassion

Every session starts by regulating first — a calm child is a child who can learn.

Inclusion

We work alongside mainstream schools, not as a separate track away from them.

Respect

Children are informed and included in decisions about their own therapy wherever possible.

Growth

Progress is measured in the child's own terms, tracked and reviewed every eight weeks.

Family Partnership

Parents sit in the same review meetings as our clinicians — not a summary afterwards.
