Facilities & Infrastructure

Classrooms

12 spacious, sensory-friendly classrooms with smart boards and natural lighting. Each room is equipped with acoustic dampening and flexible seating.

Therapy Centres

Dedicated speech therapy, occupational therapy, and sensory integration rooms with specialised equipment and trained support staff.

Library & Resources

Over 2,000 titles including picture books, braille materials, sensory story kits, and digital learning resources for diverse learners.

Outdoor Play Area

Secure, wheelchair-accessible playground with sensory garden, trampoline, and adaptive sports equipment for all ability levels.

Health & Safety

In-house nurse, regular health screenings, fire safety systems, CCTV monitoring, and emergency evacuation protocols for special needs.

Transport

GPS-tracked, wheelchair-accessible school transport covering Kozhikode, Wayanad, and Malappuram with trained attendants on every route.
