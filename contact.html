Common questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What age groups do you accept?

We work with children from 18 months through 16 years, with programs tailored to each developmental stage.

Do I need a formal diagnosis before booking an assessment?

No. Many families come to us with concerns but no formal diagnosis. Our intake team can guide you through the assessment and, if needed, refer you for a clinical diagnosis.

How long is the waitlist?

Initial assessments are typically scheduled within two weeks. Ongoing therapy start dates depend on the specific program and specialist availability.

Do you offer online or hybrid sessions?

Yes, speech therapy, parent training, and some behaviour therapy sessions are available online for families outside Kozhikode.

Is financial assistance available?

We offer a sliding-scale fee structure for qualifying families. Ask our intake coordinator during your first call.
