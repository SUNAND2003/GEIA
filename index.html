Who we are

Inspiring Confidence, Independence & Success

At GEIA Inclusive Academy, we believe every child has unique strengths and limitless potential. As a trusted special needs school UAE, we provide personalized learning, therapeutic support, and a nurturing environment where children can grow with confidence.

Recognized as a leading School for Children with special needs UAE, we offer comprehensive special education UAE, speech therapy, occupational therapy, behaviour therapy, and early intervention programs tailored to each child's individual needs. Through inclusive education UAE, our experienced team helps children develop communication, independence, social skills, and academic abilities.

As a dedicated Disability Support School UAE, we partner with families to create meaningful learning experiences that empower every child to succeed today and thrive in the future.

Our Programs

Eight connected programs, one individualized plan for your child.

Autism Support

Supporting children with autism through personalized learning, structured routines, and therapy that encourages communication, confidence, and everyday independence.

Early Intervention

Early support for young children to strengthen learning, communication, behaviour, and social development during the most important years of growth.

Special Education

Our special education UAE programs are tailored to each child's abilities, helping them learn, grow, and achieve meaningful milestones at their own pace.

Speech & Language Therapy

Helping children express themselves with confidence by improving speech, language, understanding, and social communication.

Occupational Therapy

Building motor skills, sensory awareness, coordination, and independence to make everyday activities easier and more enjoyable.

Behaviour Therapy

Supporting positive behaviour, emotional wellbeing, and social skills through practical, child-focused guidance and encouragement.

Individual Learning Plans

As a trusted school for Children with Special Needs UAE, we create learning plans that reflect each child's strengths, goals, and progress.

Parent Support

We work hand in hand with families, sharing practical guidance and regular updates to support every child's journey beyond the classroom.
