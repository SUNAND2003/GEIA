Who we are

A learning home built around the way your child actually learns

GEIA Inclusive Academy brings together special educators, speech-language pathologists, occupational therapists and behaviour specialists under one roof. Instead of asking children to fit a fixed curriculum, we build the curriculum around each child — their pace, their sensory needs, and their strengths.

Every family begins with a full developmental assessment, and every plan is reviewed and adjusted every eight weeks as your child grows.
